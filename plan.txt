# Research Paper Analysis Agent

An AI-powered research assistant that analyzes academic papers and generates structured insights from one or multiple research documents.

The system combines Retrieval-Augmented Generation (RAG) with Agentic AI workflows to help users quickly understand complex research papers without reading them in full.

Users can upload one or more papers and ask questions such as:

* Summarize this paper.
* What methodology was used?
* What are the main findings?
* What limitations were identified?
* Compare these two papers.

The agent extracts relevant sections, retrieves supporting evidence from the uploaded documents, and generates concise, structured responses with references to the original content.

### Key Features

* Research paper summarization
* Methodology extraction
* Key findings analysis
* Limitation identification
* Multi-paper comparison
* Literature review support
* Source-backed responses

### Technologies

Python, FastAPI, LangGraph, Vector Database (FAISS/ChromaDB), LLM APIs, RAG Pipeline

### Business Value

Helps researchers, students, analysts, and professionals accelerate literature review, identify important insights, and compare research findings efficiently.
